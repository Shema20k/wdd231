Discover Rwanda

Tourism & Cultural Guide — Site Plan

Site Name

Discover Rwanda

The site name was selected because it clearly communicates the purpose of the website: helping users explore Rwanda’s destinations, culture, and travel experiences. The name is simple, memorable, and appropriate for branding.

Optional domain: discoverrwanda.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide helpful and reliable information about Rwanda for tourists, students, and first-time visitors. The site will feature popular destinations, cultural highlights, travel tips, and general country information.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in Rwanda for a first-time traveler?
  • When is the best time of year to travel to Rwanda?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#1b5e20): Used for headings and section titles
  • Gold (#fbc02d): Used for accents and borders
  • Light Gray (#f9f9f9): Used for the page background

Typography

  • System UI font stack: Used for both headings and body text to ensure fast loading, accessibility, and a consistent appearance across devices.
